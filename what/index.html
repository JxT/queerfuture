What is QueerFuture?

QueerFuture is the name of workshops being run in Nottingham this August.

Queer people over 18 are invited to imagine an empowered queer tech future

This is part of a study.

These workshops will feature photo, video and audio recording.

This data will contribute to an MSc study on queer participatory design. Information sheet Privacy notice
